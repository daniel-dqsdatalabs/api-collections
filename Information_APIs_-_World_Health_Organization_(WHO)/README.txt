WHO works worldwide to promote health, keep the world safe, and serve the vulnerable. Our goal is to ensure that a billion more people have universal health coverage, to protect a billion more people from health emergencies, and provide a further billion people with better health and well-being.

This is a Postman collection providing access to the Twitter feed, Youtube channel information, and RSS feeds for the World Health Organization (ECDC), providing a one steop collection for pulling relevant information about COVID-19 / Corona Virus.

To use, you will need to setup your own [Twitter authentication in Postman](https://community.postman.com/t/twitter-api-authorization/9512), and provide your own [YoutTube API keys](https://developers.google.com/youtube/v3/getting-started).

Website - https://www.who.int/
