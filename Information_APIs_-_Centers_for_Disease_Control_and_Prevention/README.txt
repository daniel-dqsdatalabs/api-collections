This is a Postman collection providing access to the Twitter feed, Youtube channel information, and RSS feeds for the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), providing a one steop collection for pulling relevant information about COVID-19 / Corona Virus.

To use, you will need to setup your own [Twitter authentication in Postman](https://community.postman.com/t/twitter-api-authorization/9512), and provide your own [YoutTube API keys](https://developers.google.com/youtube/v3/getting-started).
